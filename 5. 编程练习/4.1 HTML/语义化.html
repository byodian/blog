"WWW" and "The Web" redirect here. For other uses of WWW, see WWW.

Not to be confused with the Internet

The World Wide Web,commonly known as the Web.

English scientist Tim Berners-Lee invensted the World Wide Web in Switzerland. [1][2]

The terms Internet and World Wide Web are often used.The Internet is a global system of interconnected computer networks. In contrast, the World Wide Web is a global collection of documents and other resources, linked by hyperlinks and URIs.
